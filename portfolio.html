They concluded that working out three times a week wasn’t enough to offset the adverse effects of sitting eight hours a day at work. Ergonomists prescribe an hourly 10 minute walk when sitting but we knew this wasn’t realistic, especially in the office environment. Five years later, SitFlow™ was born.
